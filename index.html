About Me

I'm an aspiring IT professional with a passion for technology and problem-solving.

I recently obtained an IT Helpdesk Administrator certification. I'm currently working toward two additional COMPTIA certifications with exams/completion slated for summer/fall 2025. My goal with these certifications is to enhance my skills and knowledge in the IT field and transition into an IT career.

This website was created over one weekend to demonstrate my interest in IT, my dedication to learning new IT-related things, and my ability to implement those things successfully. The site will, over time, also serve as a hub for my IT journey, including a blog where I share tutorials and updates, as well as my goals.

I have a decade of broad experience in patient-facing healthcare. I am now pivoting to IT, where I can leverage my skills and experience to help others with their technology needs.

I grew up in a tech-focused household with a parent in Software Development who ensured I had early access to PC's and the internet at a time when these technologies were not yet widely adopted. I believe my specific combination of professional experience, upbringing, personal experience and interests, and current focus on COMPTIA certifications, gives me a unique understanding of technology in healthcare that I can apply to my work in the tech industry.

I am a philomath by nature, which fuels my desire to develop a deeper understanding of things that impact me and things I find interesting. This includes gardening and landscaping, DIY home improvements, health, science, technology, and many other things. I also treasure spending time with my family and pets.
